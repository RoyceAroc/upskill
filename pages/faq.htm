General FAQs

How do I submit submissions?

There will be a dropbox for submissions. The dropbox will be under the information for the current competition. For some events, you might need an account, so remember to read the rules and requirements!

General FAQs

I am having trouble submitting my submission, what can I do?

Email us at upskillcompetitioncontactsubmissions@gmail.org. Explain that your submission will not submit through the dropbox. However, we will still uphold the deadline meaning if it is submitted after the deadline, the submissions will not be accepted for judging.

General FAQs

How do I cancel my membership fee for the classes?

When you click on my account, and scroll down to the bottom, there will be an option to cancel membership. Click that and your membership will be canceled.

Is creating an account free?

Yes, it is free. However, if you want a membership, you have to pay $100 per month. When you have a membership, you have access to a variety of classes.
