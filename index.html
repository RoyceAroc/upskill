Competition Insights

Our competitions will include challenges related to academics for those interested and related to arts if one is not into studious fields. This month's competition will test the academic intelligence of artistic abilities. Each month we will hold a competition related to a different field. For the month of March, we will be holding a virtual art competition. Our competitions will include prizes ranging from discounts on our online courses to cash scholarships. We greatly encourage participation in our competitions as these will help one grow and find or nurture their academic or artistic talents.
